Chicken Gnocchi Soup

Description

My partners mimic of the Chicken Gnocchi soup served at Olive Garden.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package of Gnocchi
  • 3 handfuls of Baby Spinach
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Thyme
  • Olive Oil
  • 3 Chicken Breasts
  • 1/2 of a Diced White Onion
  • 2 Stalks of Celery
  • 2 Carrots Diced
  • 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 Cups Chicken Broth

Directions

  1. Cook Chicken seasoned with salt and pepper
  2. Dice chicken into bite size pieces
  3. Dice Vegetables
  4. In a large bot add 2 tbsp olive oil
  5. On medium heat add veggies and saute till onions are translucent
  6. Add chicken and broth
  7. Bring to boil over high heat
  8. Boil for 10 minutes or until Carrots and Celery are tender
  9. Add pepper, salt, and thyme
  10. Add gnocchi
  11. Stir on medium heat, simmer for 5-6 minutes or until tender
  12. Add heavy cream and spinach
  13. Cook for another 2-3 minutes
  14. Serve
