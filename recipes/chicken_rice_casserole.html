Chicken Rice Casserole

Description

A simple to make casserole dish made with rice, chicken, broth, and cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Chicken Broth
  • 2 Cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 Cups Minute Rice
  • 3 Chicken Breasts Precooked
  • Cheddar for on top

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Precook Chicken
  3. Add cans of soup, rice, and broth in a large mixing bowl
  4. Shred Chicken
  5. Add chicken to the mixing bowl
  6. Stir till well mixed
  7. Spread mixture into 7x11 baking dish
  8. Shread cheddar on top
  9. Bake in oven for 45 minutes
  10. Serve
