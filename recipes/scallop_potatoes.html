Scallop Potatoes

Description

A tasty dish of cheesy scalloped potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Thinly Sliced Potatoes
  • 3 Tablespoons Butter
  • 3 Tablespoons Flour
  • 1 1/2 Cups Milk
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 Dash Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Cup Grated Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Cheddar Cheese for topping
  • Paprika for color

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In a small saucepan, melt butter and blend in flour
  3. Let sit for a minute
  4. Add all of cold milk, stirring with a whisk
  5. Season with salt and cayenne
  6. Cook sauce on low until smooth and boiling, stirring occasionally with a whisk
  7. Reduce heat and stir in cheese
  8. Place half of the sliced potatoes in a lightly greased one quart casserole dish
  9. Pour half of the cheese sauce over the potatoes
  10. Repeat with second layer of potatoes and cheese sauce
  11. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top
  12. Top with paprika for color
  13. Bake uncovered for about 1 hour at 350F
